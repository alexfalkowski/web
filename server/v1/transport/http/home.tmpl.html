Lean Thinking

“The most dangerous kind of waste is the waste we do not recognize.”
— Shigeo Shingo

Lean thinking encourages teams to visualize, manage, and continuously optimize their flow. If the goal is to deliver value to the customer at a sustainably fast pace, then we must have control over how work gets done; we must manage flow.
